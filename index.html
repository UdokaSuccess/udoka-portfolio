The why

Nigeria's emergency health system has a documentation and information-sharing problem. Patients are always confused on which hospitals to go especially due to infrastructure, blood availability etc. Emergent is being built to change that, we would make sure that every Nigerian in an emergency knows where exactly to go, and never loses a life due to lack of information.
